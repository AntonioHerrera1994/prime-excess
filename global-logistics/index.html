Customs Process

Complete Customs Brokerage Services

We handle every step of the Mexican customs process — from pedimento preparation to cargo release — with licensed agentes aduanales and cutting-edge compliance technology.

01

Pedimento & VUCEM Filing

Electronic filing of import/export declarations in Mexico's Ventanilla Única de Comercio Exterior Mexicano with direct link to customs authorities.

02

NOM & Permit Management

Compliance with all Official Mexican Standards (NOMs) and sector-specific import permits including health, safety, and environmental regulations.

03

Padron de Importadores Registration

Registration and ongoing maintenance in Mexico's official importer registry, including sector-specific padrons for restricted goods.

04

Customs Audit Defense

Expert representation and documentation support during AGACE audits, customs verifications, and duty dispute resolution proceedings.
