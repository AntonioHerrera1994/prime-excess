Heavy Machinery & Operations

Industrial Equipment

From manufacturing lines to decommissioned plant machinery, we manage the full lifecycle of industrial assets — ensuring nothing of value goes unrecovered.

Manufacturing & Production
CNC machines, lathes, presses, conveyors, and production-line equipment. Assets are assessed for resale value in secondary industrial markets or redirected to facilities with active demand for specific machinery types.
Heavy Tools & Worksite Assets
Forklifts, hoists, cranes, compressors, generators, and specialized worksite equipment. We evaluate condition, operational history, and market demand to determine the optimal recovery channel for each asset.
Facility & Infrastructure Equipment
HVAC systems, transformers, electrical panels, pumps, and facility infrastructure. These assets often retain 40–60% of original value through targeted buyer outreach and industrial auction platforms.
Overstock & Warehouse Management

Surplus Inventory

Idle inventory ties up capital and space. We convert warehouse overflow into recovered value through structured disposition strategies designed around your business timeline.

Raw Materials & Overstock
Excess raw materials, unused components, MRO parts, and overstock that accumulated during production cycles. We connect materials to buyers across manufacturing and supply chain networks to maximize per-unit recovery.
Finished Goods & Seasonal Inventory
Unsold finished goods, seasonal items, and discontinued product lines. Through B2B consignment, auction platforms, and direct sales networks, we recover value while clearing warehouse space efficiently.
Warehouse & Storage Infrastructure
Pallet racking, shelving systems, material handling equipment, and storage infrastructure. Often overlooked, these assets retain strong resale value and are in consistent demand across warehouse and logistics operations.
IT & Technology Assets

Technology & Electronics

Outdated technology doesn't mean zero value. Enterprise IT assets, lab instruments, and electronics retain recoverable value long past their useful life in your organization.

Enterprise IT & Servers
Servers, networking equipment, storage arrays, and enterprise computing infrastructure. We identify refurbishment potential, component-level value, and secondary market buyers to recover maximum value from decommissioned IT environments.
Lab & Test Equipment
Oscilloscopes, signal analyzers, testing instruments, and precision lab equipment. These specialized assets command premium prices in secondary markets and are actively sought by research institutions and engineering firms.
Consumer & Commercial Electronics
Surplus commercial electronics, point-of-sale systems, displays, and AV equipment. We leverage direct sales channels, eBay remarketing, and specialized electronics buyers to recover value across all condition tiers.
