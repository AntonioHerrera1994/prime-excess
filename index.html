About Prime Excess

Built for companies that want to
work smarter with their assets.

Whether you manage a single facility or multiple operations, we help you recover maximum value from surplus assets — so your organization can focus entirely on building its infrastructure. Every asset redirected becomes an act of patronage: a resource that empowers a new generation of entrepreneurs to carry The Legacy forward with precision, care, and integrity.

Industrial Equipment

Manufacturing machinery, production lines, heavy tools, and specialized industrial assets — we identify, assess, and recover the full value of your operational equipment at any scale or condition.

Surplus Management

Excess inventory, overstock, and warehouse overflow represent locked capital. We build custom disposition strategies — from direct resale to auction platforms — that maximize your recovery rate and free operational space.

Technology & Electronics

Outdated servers, networking equipment, lab instruments, and enterprise technology retain significant value long after they leave active inventory. We recover it through strategic remarketing and repurposing.

Entrepreneur Support

We believe surplus assets are the physical infrastructure of the next entrepreneurial wave. We channel equipment, technology, and inventory toward an ecosystem of emerging builders — lowering the barrier to entry so talent, not capital, determines who gets to compete.
